Vvardenfell is the home of the Dark Elves, who live in giant mushrooms and ride massive insects. The Dark Elves are run by the Tribunal a group of mortals turn God who froze a falling asteroid and turned it into a prison. The ash covered world feels ancient and strange. Morrowind is generally considered the most exotic and lore rich in the series.
