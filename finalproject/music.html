My Music History

I have loved music for a long time. I started in 4th grade playing trumpet. In middle school I learned french Hornand drumset. I also started playing ukulele. When Highschool rolled around I picked up Mellphone, alto bugle, FLugel Horn, and Guitar. When I went on my mission I picked up a couple guitars and an awsome companion that convinced me to start writing songs myself. Ill always be greateful for him shout out to Paxton. After the mission I continued to write music learn a little bit of piano and play at a few local venues.
